## 1. Transport & Global Controls
The top bar contains the main controls for playback and project management.

![Transport Controls](assets/manual-transport.png)

*   **RUN / STOP**: Starts and stops the audio sequencer.
*   **RANDOMIZE**: Instantly generates a completely new random pattern and sound patch for all units. Perfect for finding inspiration!
*   **CLEAR**: Resets all patterns (303 notes and 909 drums) to a blank state.
*   **SHARE URL**: Generates a unique link containing your current pattern and settings. The link is copied to your clipboard so you can share your beat with others.
*   **TEMPO**: Controls the speed of the track (BPM).
    *   **Drag**: Click and drag the large knob to adjust.
    *   **Reset**: Double-click the knob to reset to 125 BPM.

## 2. TB-303 Bassline Units
AcidBros features **two independent TB-303 units** (Unit 1 & Unit 2). These synthesize the iconic "acid" basslines and leads.

![TB-303 Unit](assets/manual-tb303.png)

### Sound Controls (Knobs & Switches)
*   **WAVEFORM**: Toggle between two classic shapes:
    *   **Sawtooth (Left)**: Sharp, buzzy, and aggressive.
    *   **Square (Right)**: Hollow, woody, and deep.
*   **TUNING**: Fine-tunes the pitch of the synthesizer.
*   **CUTOFF**: Controls the brightness. Turn it up to open the filter.
*   **RESONANCE**: The most important knob! Controls the "squelch" or "whistling" character of the filter.
*   **ENV MOD**: Determines how much the filter envelope affects the Cutoff frequency.
*   **DECAY**: Controls how fast the sound fades out.
*   **ACCENT**: Sets the intensity of accented steps (louder and punchier).
*   **VOLUME**: Adjusts the output level of the unit.
*   ***Pro Tip**: Double-click any knob to reset it to its default position.*

### The Sequencer
The 16-step grid represents one bar of music.
*   **Toggle Step**: Click a step button (LED) to turn it **ON** (Red) or **OFF** (Dark).
*   **Note Editor**: Click the **Note Display** (e.g., "C 2") above a step to open the **Piano Roll Popover**.

#### Piano Roll & Step Options
When you click a note display, a popover appears:

![Piano Roll Popover](assets/manual-pianoroll.png)

*   **Piano Keys**: Select the pitch for the step visually.
*   **Octave (DN/UP)**: Shift the note down or up by one octave.
*   **AC (Accent)**: Adds emphasis to the step (triggers the Accent knob).
*   **SL (Slide)**: Glides the pitch to the next note (Legato effect).
*   **Preview**: Check "Preview Sound" to hear the note as you modify it.

## 3. TR-909 Rhythm Composer
The TR-909 section handles your drums and percussion.

![TR-909 Unit](assets/manual-tr909.png)

### Drum Tracks
*   **BASS DRUM (BD)**: The kick drum. Controls for Tune, Decay, Attack, and Level.
*   **SNARE DRUM (SD)**: The snare. Controls for Tune, Tone, Snappy (noise), and Level.
*   **CLOSED HAT (CH)**: Short hi-hat. Controls for Decay and Level.
*   **OPEN HAT (OH)**: Long hi-hat. Controls for Decay and Level.
*   **CLAP (CP)**: Hand clap. Controls for Decay and Level.

### Sequencing Drums
*   Click the **16-step buttons** in each row to program the rhythm.
*   **Active Step**: Lights up Orange/Yellow.
*   **Inactive Step**: Dark.

## 4. Common UI Interactions
Learn how to interact with the knobs and controls efficiently.

*   **Adjusting Knobs**: Click (or touch) a knob and drag up or down to change its value.
*   **Resetting Knobs**: Double-click (or double-tap) any knob to instantly reset it to its default position. This works for all sound parameters and the Tempo control.
*   **Preventing Zoom**: On mobile devices, the interface is locked to prevent accidental zooming, allowing you to tap rapidly on the sequencer steps.

---

## 5. Quick Start Guide
1.  Press **RANDOMIZE** to get a starting point.
2.  Press **RUN** to hear the beat.
3.  Tweak the **CUTOFF** and **RESONANCE** knobs on the TB-303s while playing.
4.  Click **CLEAR** if you want to build a beat from scratch.
5.  Click **SHARE URL** to save your creation and send it to a friend!
